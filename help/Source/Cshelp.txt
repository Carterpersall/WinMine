.topic IDH_CUSTOM_HEIGHT
Specifies the number of vertical squares on the playing field.

.topic IDH_CUSTOM_WIDTH
Specifies the number of horizontal squares on the playing field.

.topic IDH_CUSTOM_MINES
Specifies the number of mines to be placed on the playing field.

.topic IDH_BESTTIMES_BTN_RESET
Click to clear the current high scores.

.topic IDH_BESTTIMES_DISPLAY
Displays a player's best game time, and the player's name, for each level of play: Beginner, Intermediate, and Expert.
