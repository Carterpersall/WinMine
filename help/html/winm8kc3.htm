Strategy and Hints

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This section contains helpful hints for playing Minesweeper successfully.

Use the number squares wisely. If a square contains a 2, and there are only two squares in the surrounding eight which are still uncovered, they must be mines.

It's clear that the two uncovered squares are mines.

Don't guess. If you can't figure out how to move, try approaching the area from a different direction.
